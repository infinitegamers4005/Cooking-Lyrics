Caramel Custard
  • Serve : 8 People
  • Time: Prep: 15 min. Bake: 40 min.

Served warm or chilled, this caramel-glazed classic is both a comforting and elegant dessert!

  
 
 

Ingredients
 
 
  • 50grams / 4 Tablespoons Sugar
  • 350ml / 1.5 Cups Milk
  • 2 Eggs + 1 Egg Egg Yolk
 
  • 25grams / 2 Tablespoons Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 500ml / 1 & 3/4 cups Water
 

Preparation

  1. In a large heavy saucepan, cook and stir 3/4 cup sugar over low heat until sugar is melted and golden. Pour into eight 6-oz. custard cups, tilting to coat bottom of cup; let stand for 10 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, beat the eggs, milk, vanilla and remaining sugar until blended but not foamy. Pour over caramelized sugar.
  3. Place the cups in two 8-in. square baking pans. Pour very hot water in pans to a depth of 1 in. Bake at 350Â° until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean, 40-45 minutes. Remove from pans to cool on wire racks.
  4. To unmold, run a knife around rim of cup and invert onto dessert place. Serve warm or chilled.
