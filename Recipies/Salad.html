Salad

By Flavourofdesifood


  • Serve : 4 to 5 People
  • Time : 5 minutes

A salad is a dish consisting of mixed pieces of food, sometimes with at least one raw ingredient. It is often dressed, and is typically served at room temperature or chilled, though some can be served warm.

  
 
 

Ingredients
 
  • 2 Cucumbers
  • 2 Carrots
  • 1 Onion
  • 1/2 Cabbage
  • 2 Tbsp Pomegranate
  • 1/2 Green and Yellow Capsicum
  • 1/4 litre Lemon Juice
  • 1 tsp Herbs
  • 1/4 Green apple
  • 1/2 Green Coriander
 

Preparation

  1. Add every vegetable given above chopped precisely in a mixing bowl (except pomegranate, lemon juice, herbs)
  2. Take a cup and add lemon juice and herbs and mix it well, then add it to the bowl.
  3. Add salt and pepper to it and the salad is ready to serve.
