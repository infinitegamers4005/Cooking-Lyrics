Chole Bhature

By Sanjyot Keer

  • Serve : 4 People
  • Time : 2 Hours 25 Mins

A popular Punjabi dish made with chole (chickpeas) and bhature (deep fried bread).

 
 
 

Ingredients
 

    For Pressure Cooking Chole :

  • 1 cup Chana / Chickpea (Soaked Overnight)
  • 2 Tea Bags
  • 1/4 Tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  • 3 Cup Water

    • For the Chole preperation :

  • 2 Tbsp Oil
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • 1 black cardamom
  • 2 Pod Cardamom
  • 1 Inch Cinnamon Stick
  • 1 Tsp Cumin
  • 1/2 Tsp Kasuri Methi
  • 1 Onion(Finely Chopped)
  • 1 tsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1/4 Tsp Turmeric
  • 1 Tsp Chilli Powder
  • 1 Tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 Tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1/2 Tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 Tsp Aamchur
  • 1/4 Tsp Salt
  • 1 and 1/2 Cup tomato Puree
  • 2 Tbsp Coriander(Finely Chopped)

    • For Tempering:

  • 1 Tbsp Ghee
  • 2 Chilli(Slit)
  • 1/4 Tsp Turmeric
  • 1/4 Tsp Chilli Powder
  • 1/4 Tsp Garam Masala

    • For Bhatura:

  • 2 Cup Maida
  • 2 Tbsp Rava / Semolina (fine)
  • 1 Tsp Sugar
  • 1/4 Tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt
  • 2 Tbsp Oil
  • 1/4 Cup Curd
  • For Kneading Water
  • For Frying Oil
 

Preparation

    How to make Bhatura Recipe :

  1. firstly, in a large bowl take 2 cup maida, 2 tbsp rava, 1 tsp sugar, Â¼ tsp baking soda, 1 tsp sugar, Â½ tsp salt and 2 tbsp oil. mix well.
  2. now add Â¼ cup curd and mix well making sure everything is well combined.
  3. further, add water as required and knead the dough.
  4. knead to the smooth and soft dough without putting much pressure.
  5. grease the dough with oil, cover and rest for 2 hours
  6. after 2 hours, knead the dough slightly.
  7. pinch a ball sized dough and make a ball without andy cracks.
  8. roll slightly thick, applying oil to prevent from sticking.
  9. drop the rolled dough into the hot oil.
  10. press until the bhature puffs up and splash oil to puff up fully.
  11. flip over and fry until it turns golden brown.
  12. finally, drain off the bhatura and is ready to enjoy with chole masala.
