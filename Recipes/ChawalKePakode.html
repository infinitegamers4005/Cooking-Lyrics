Chawal Ke Pakode

By NishaMadhulika

  • Serve : 4 People
  • Time : 30 minutes

Pakode is loved by all in India and if they are made of leftover rice, it tastes more delicious. The leftover rice of afternoon can be used as evening snacks with this recipe.

  
 
 

Ingredients
 
  • 2 cups Cooked rice
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 2 green chillies chopped
  • 1/2 inch long piece (grated) Ginger
  • 1/4 tsp Amchoor powder
  • 1/4 tsp Red Chilli powder
  • 1 Tbsp Coriander leaves
  • 1 cup Gram flour
  • 1/2 tsp Coriander powder
  • Oil for Frying
 

Preparation

  1. Mix salt, green chilli, ginger, amchoor powder, red chilli powder and coriander leaves into pre-cooked rice. Take out a small portion from the mixture equal to the size of 1 lemon. Press it with both hands and flatten it. Give it a round or an oval shape as per your desire. Make as many such balls from the mixture and keep them on a plate.
  2. Sieve the gram flour and put salt, red chilli powder, coriander powder into it. Make a batter of the mixture by adding water into it. Whisk the mixture with a spoon for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Take one rice ball and dip it into the batter. Put the pakoda into the pan and fry it till it turns brown in colour. Likewise, make 4-5 pakodas and fry them at one time. Take out the fried pakodas on the plate over a napkin. Prepare all the other pakodas in the same way.
  4. Serve the hot crispy Rice Pakodas with coriander chutney, chilly sauce and tomato ketchup.
