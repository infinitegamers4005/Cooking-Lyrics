Rava Appe

By WhiskAffair

  • Serve : 4 People
  • Time : 30 minutes

Rava Appe (Suji Appe, Rava Paniyaram, Rava Paddu) is an Instant South Indian breakfast dish that is delicious and very easy to make.

  
 
 

Ingredients
 
  • 2 Tbsp Vegetable Oil (plus for frying)
  • 1/2 Tsp Brown Mustard Seeds
  • 1/2 Tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 Tsp Bengal Gram (Chana Dal)
  • 1/4 Tsp Asfetida (Hing)
  • 10-12 Curry Leaves
  • 1 Tsp Ginger (Finely Chopped)
  • 1 Tsp Green Chilli (Finely Chopped)
  • 1 Dry Red Chilli(Broken into small pieces)
  • 4-5 Whole Cashew Nuts
  • 1 Cup Fine Rava (Semolina,Sooji)
  • 1/2 Cup Plain Yogurt (Dahi, Curd)
  • 2 Tbsp Grated Carrots
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp ENO fruit salt (or 1 teaspoon baking soda + 1 teaspoon lime juice)
 

Preparation

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Once the oil is hot, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and chana dal and fry for 5-6 seconds.
  3. Add asafetida, curry leaves, ginger, green chilies, dry red chili, and 4-5 whole cashew nuts, and fry until cashew nuts turn slightly brown. Stir frequently while frying.
  4. Reduce the heat to low.
  5. Now add rava to the pan and roast it until slightly browned (3-4 minutes). Stir continuously while roasting to avoid the rava from burning at the bottom of the pan.
  6. Remove the pan from the heat and let the rava cool down completely.
  7. Transfer the cooled roasted rava to a large mixing bowl.
  8. Add yogurt and carrot to the bowl and mix well.
  9. Add Â½ cup of water and mix well.
  10. Cover the bowl with a lid and keep it aside for 15 minutes.
  11. The rava will absorb the water during the resting time and the batter will become thick.
  12. Add 2-3 tablespoons of water to the batter and mix well. We need an idli-like batter to make the appe.
  13. Now add ENO fruit salt and salt and mix well.
  14. Heat an appe pan over medium heat.
  15. Add Â¼ teaspoon of vegetable oil to each cavity.
  16. Using a spoon, pour the batter into each cavity. Do not overfill.
  17. Cover the pan with a lid and cook until the appe are nicely browned from the bottom.
  18. Flip using a spoon and cook uncovered from the other side for 2-3 minutes.
  19. Remove the rava appe from the pan and serve hot.
