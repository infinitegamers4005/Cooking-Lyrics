Rasgulla

By Taste Unfold

  • Serve : 8 People
  • Time: Prep: 15 min. Bake: 40 min.

Rasgulla is a Bengali sweet which is famous all around India. Bengali Rasgulla recipe is super easy to make at home. It has chenna or paneer and it is prepared by boiling paneer balls in sugar syrup. These are also packed with protein and considered to be a healthy sweet option.

 
 
 

Ingredients
 
 
  • 1-1.5 ltrs Milk
  • 250 grams Sugar
  • Lemon Juice
  • Maida
  • Elaichi
 
 

Preparation

  1. In a vessel heat some milk.
  2. Put the lemon juice in the heated milk and mix well
  3. Let the milk and the lemon juice mixed garnish for some time
  4. Place a cloth on a big vessel and put your garnished milk and lemon juice on the cloth and squeeze to remove the water out
  5. Wash the garnished milk and lemon juice with cold water to remove the taste of bitterness of the lemon juice.
  6. Remove the water again
  7. Take a big vessel and mix the challa
  8. Add some maida to it and mix well
  9. Make some balls out of the challa
  10. Take a vessel and add some sugar in it and mix well. Start to boil it.
  11. After it starts boiling you then put some elaichi to get elaichi flavour and put the bals which you have made in the boiling water and elaichi.
  12. Put the gas on high flame and wait for 5 minutes
  13. After 5 minutes, flip them and wait for 5 minutes more to properly fry.
  14. If properly fried, put it in the fridge to cool down.
  15. After cooling it, your rasgullas are ready to serve
