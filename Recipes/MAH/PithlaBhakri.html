Pithala Bhakri

By Meghna's Food Magic

  • Serve : 8 servings
  • Time : 1 hour 15 minutes

If you love authentic Maharashtrian cuisine, then you have to try this Pithla Bhakri recipe. Don't worry if this is your first time preparing this dish, as this super quick and easy Pithla bhakri recipe with step by step instructions will help you.

  
 
 

Ingredients
 

    For Pithala

  • 1 Cup Besan (Gram Flour)
  • 1 Tsp Turmeric Powder (Haldi)
  • Salt as per taste
  • Pinch of Hing (Asafoetida)
  • 1.5 Cup Water
  • 2 Tbsp Oil
  • 1 Tsp Rai (Mustard Seeds)
  • 1 Tsp Jeera (Cumin Seeds)
  • 3 Garlic Cloves (chopped)
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 2-3 Green Chillies (chopped)
  • 10-12 Kadi Patta (Curry Leaves)
  • Some Fresh Coriander as per required

    • For Jowar Bhakri

  • 1 cup Jowar atta (Sorghum)
  • As required Salt as per taste
  • 1/2 cup or As required Warm water
 

Preparation

For Pithala

  1. In a bowl, add besan, turmeric powder, salt ,hing and water and Mix it well.
  2. Use 1 and half cup water keep it aside.
  3. Heat a pan and add oil, rai, jeera, garlic , onion, green chillies, curry leaves
  4. Sauté till onion are cooked
  5. Now add besan mixture and stir
  6. Cook on Medium heat
  7. Continuously cook it for 7-8 minutes
  8. Now add fresh coriander mix it well .

For Bhakri

  1. In a plate, add jowar atta, salt as per taste and make medium soft dough using warm water.
  2. Take 1 portion of Dough and sprinkle some jowar atta on the plate
  3. Now flatten dough using your hand and make bhakri.
  4. Heat a Tawa nicely and and put bhakari on tawa
  5. Apply some water and spread immediately
  6. Water helps prevent cracks in bhakhri.
  7. Cook from both side
  8. Pithala & Bhakhri is ready; serve with onion and enjoy.
