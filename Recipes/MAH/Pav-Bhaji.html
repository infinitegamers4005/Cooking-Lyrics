Pav Bhaji

By HomeCookingShow

  • Serve : 4-5 People
  • Total Time : 60 minutes

Pav Bhaji Recipe - it is a fast-food dish from the Indian state of Maharashtra. It is a vegetable-based curry served with a soft bread roll called pav.

 
 
 

Ingredients
 
  • Potato - 3 Nos
  • Carrot - 3 Nos
  • Green Peas - 1 Cup
  • Cauliflower - 1 No.
  • Salt - As Per Required
  • Turmeric Powder - 1/2 Tsp
  • Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder - 3 Tsp
  • Water - 2 1/2 Cups
  • Unsalted Butter - 100 Gms
  • Oil - 2 Tbsp
  • Cumin Seeds - 1 Tsp
  • Onion - 3 Nos
  • Green Chilli - 3 Nos
  • Ginger Garlic Paste - 2 Tsp
  • Tomato - 3 Nos Chopped
  • Pav Bhaji Masala - 4 Tsp
  • Capsicum - 1 No.
 

Preparation

  1. Take potatoes, carrot, green peas and cauliflower in a pressure cooker.
  2. Put salt, turmeric powder and red chili powder with them and add 2 cups of water.
  3. Pressure cook till 4-5 whistles on medium flame and mash the veggies. Keep it aside.
  4. Take unsalted butter in an iron tawa. Add oil and cumin seeds. Saute.
  5. Add onions and saute on high flame until they are golden brown in colour.
  6. Add green chilies and ginger garlic paste. Saute till the raw smell is gone.
  7. Add tomatoes. Let them soften and add salt, red chili powder, turmeric powder and pav bhaji masala powder.
  8. Mix well and add capsicum. Cook for 3 mins.
  9. Add cooked and mashed veggies. Add 1/2 cup water and mix well.
  10. Mash the bhaji again. Add salt or water if required.
  11. Apply butter on a pan and place the pav buns on it.
  12. Roast the pav buns on both sides.
  13. Serve the street style Pav Bhaji piping hot with lemon, chopped onions, chopped coriander and butter.
