Tomato Soup

By Cooking with Reshu


  • Serve : 4 to 5 People
  • Time : 5 minutes

Tomato soup is a soup made with tomatoes as the primary ingredient. It may be served hot or cold in a bowl, and may be made in a variety of ways.

  
 
 

Ingredients
 
  • 5 Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 tsp Herbs
  • 1 tsp Spices
  • 3 Tbsp Corn flour
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 2 Basil leaves
 

Preparation

  1. Pour one tablespoon of oil or butter and then add 1 small bay leaf, garlic cloves and chopped onions.
  2. Fry them at high flame for 3 to 4 min until they are slightly roasted.
  3. Add tomatoes and also add salt.
  4. Cook them until they are soft. Add stalks of basil and cook until tomatoes and onions turn completely mushy.
  5. Cool the mixture and remove the basil and bay leaf
  6. Transfer it to a mixer and pour 1 cup water. The turn on the mixture
  7. Place a sieve over a pan and pour the tomatoes which were in the mixer and filter. Then Bring the soup to boil on medium heat.
  8. Meanwhile add corn flour and half cup water and mix well to make a slurry.
  9. When tomato soup comes to boil add the corn flour mixture and mix it.
  10. Then add sugar to it and add salt if required and when it boiled add herbs and then it ready to serve
