Khichdi Ke Kebabs

By Rajshri Food

  • Serve : 7 Cutlets
  • Time : 50 minutes

Made with dals and rice, or even daliya, khichdi is light on the stomach and is extremely nutritious and healthy. Which is why it is often the first of many go-to recipes in most Indian kitchens. However, if you have some leftover khichdi from the previous night and don’t want to have it for lunch the next day, you are in for a delicious surprise.

  
 
 

Ingredients
 
  • 1 1/2 cup Boiled rice & Moong dal (equal quantity)
  • 1 Onion grated
  • 1 Tomato grated
  • 2 Green chillies finely chopped
  • 1 tsp Garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup Yellow corn boiled
  • 1 cup Bread crumbs
  • 1/2 tsp Chana masala
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder (haldi)
  • 1/2 tsp Garam masala
  • 1/4 tsp Red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp Mango pickle
  • Coriander leaves
  • Oil
  • Salt to Taste
 

Yogurt dressing:

  • 1 cup Hung curd
  • 2 Tbsp Mint leaves
  • Coriander leaves
  • 1/4 tsp Red chilli powder
  • 1/4 tsp Cumin powder
  • Salt
 

Preparation

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add grated onion to it. Add garlic paste.
  2. Add grated tomatoes. Cook it well till it dries.
  3. Add finely chopped green chillies, red chilli powder, garam masala, chat masala and mix well.
  4. Add plain boiled khichdi to the mixture.
  5. Add gram flour to it along with boiled yellow corn.
  6. Add salt to taste and coriander leaves.
  7. Add a pinch of mango pickle at the end.
  8. Allow it to cool down completely.
  9. Apply oil to hands and roll the mixture in to the shape of a kebab.
  10. Coat the kebab with bread crumbs.
  11. Heat oil in a pan and roast the kebabs.Kebabs is ready to serve.
